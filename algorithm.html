Algorithm for Implementation

This document outlines the steps and inputs for urban security and mobility systems, focusing on the integration of advanced technologies.

Inputs

  • CCTV Feeds: Real-time video streams for vehicle and object detection.
  • Satellite Imagery: Maps for light analysis and environmental observations.
  • IoT Sensors: Data from traffic counters, speed detectors, and environmental sensors.
  • Public Reports: Incidents and safety concerns from the public.
  • Vehicle & Citizen Databases: Preloaded data of vehicles, criminal records, and demographics.
  • Housing Prices: Property values for wealth analysis.

Outputs

  • Heatmaps: Crime density, traffic accidents, and wealth disparity.
  • Alerts: Unregistered vehicles, weapon detection, suspicious activities.
  • Information Dashboard: Visualized traffic and safety metrics for authorities.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Data Collection and Preprocessing: Gather feeds from CCTV, satellite imagery, IoT sensors, and public reports.
  2. Crime Analysis: Use OCR for license plate detection, apply computer vision for weapon detection, and use facial recognition for criminal identification.
  3. Socioeconomic Mapping: Analyze light maps for wealth zones.
  4. Traffic Accident Heatmap: Generate heatmaps for accident-prone areas.
  5. Rerouting for Women and Children: Use crime and traffic data to suggest safer routes.
  6. Data Feedback Loop: Continuously refine predictions using collected data and outcomes.

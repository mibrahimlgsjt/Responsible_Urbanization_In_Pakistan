Data Sources for Traffic Calculation

Below are the primary data sources used for traffic calculation:

  • IoT Devices (Sensors): Real-time vehicle count, speed, and density.
  • Aerial Imagery: Drone or satellite images to monitor traffic patterns.
  • GPS Data: Vehicle locations and speeds from navigation devices.
  • CCTV Cameras: Video feeds processed through machine vision for detecting congestion.

Welcome to Pakistan's Safety and Security Platform

We leverage cutting-edge technology to ensure urban safety, streamline mobility, and promote responsible urbanization.

Key Features of the Project

Real-Time Image Recognition

Monitor and identify license plates, firearms, and facial recognition for better public safety.

Data Collection

Collect and analyze data on criminal activities and traffic incidents for decision-making.

Anonymous Complaint Portal

Empower citizens to report complaints while maintaining their anonymity.

Heat Maps

Visualize crime and traffic data through interactive heat maps.

Our Mission

At the heart of the Safe City Project is the idea that a city's growth and development should enhance the well-being of all its citizens. Responsible urbanization is the key to building a future where infrastructure is well-planned, resources are efficiently used, and every resident feels secure. We believe that by using technology for good, we can transform urban life, making it safer and more accessible for everyone.

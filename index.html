Welcome to the Urban Security and Mobility System

Our goal is to create a safer, smarter, and more sustainable city by leveraging technology to improve urban mobility, public safety, and infrastructure management.

The Safe City Project aims to integrate various ICT solutions to enhance public safety, provide real-time data for emergency services, and ensure better traffic management. By promoting Responsible Urbanization, we can create a more equitable, accessible, and resilient urban environment for all.

Key Features of the Safe City Project

Real-Time License Plate Recognition (OCR)

Monitor and track vehicle movements using advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology for better law enforcement and security.

Gun Detection using Computer Vision

Enhance public safety by detecting firearms in public spaces and preventing potential threats in real-time using computer vision.

Facial Recognition for Enhanced Security

Utilize facial recognition technology to identify individuals in crowded public spaces, enhancing security efforts and improving crime prevention.

Interactive Crime Heat Map

Track criminal activity across the city with an interactive heat map that provides real-time insights into high-crime areas.

Traffic Accident and Safety Analysis

Analyze traffic accident hotspots and improve urban mobility by identifying areas with the highest rates of traffic-related incidents.

Women & Children Safety Rerouting

Provide safer routes for women and children by identifying areas of risk and guiding them to safer zones using real-time data.

Our Mission

At the heart of the Safe City Project is the idea that a city's growth and development should enhance the well-being of all its citizens. Responsible urbanization is the key to building a future where infrastructure is well-planned, resources are efficiently used, and every resident feels secure. We believe that by using technology for good, we can transform urban life, making it safer and more accessible for everyone.

How to Respond in Emergencies

Stay calm and act quickly. Contact the relevant authorities listed above for immediate assistance. For medical or fire emergencies, dial 1122. If you're traveling, the Punjab Highway Patrol (1124) and Motorway Police (130) are equipped to help with road-related incidents.

In cases of cybercrime, call 1991. For child protection services, contact the Child Protection Bureau at 1121. By utilizing these resources, you can ensure safety for yourself and others.

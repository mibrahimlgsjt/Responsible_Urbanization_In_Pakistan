In case of an emergency, it is essential to remain calm and act swiftly. Use the contact numbers listed above to connect with the appropriate authorities. For life-threatening emergencies, such as medical issues, fires, or accidents, dial 1122 to access rescue services available 24/7.

When traveling on highways, reach out to the Punjab Highway Patrol at 1124 or the Motorway Police at 130 for assistance with road accidents, breakdowns, or security concerns. For cases involving online harassment, scams, or hacking, call the Cyber Crime Department at 1991. Child safety concerns can be reported to the Child Protection Bureau at 1121. These services are designed to safeguard lives and ensure public safety across Pakistan.

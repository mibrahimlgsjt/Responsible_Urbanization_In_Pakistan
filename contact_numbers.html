How to Respond in Emergencies

During emergencies, staying calm and acting quickly can save lives. Follow these steps:

  • For medical or fire emergencies, call 1122.
  • If traveling, contact the Punjab Highway Patrol (1124) or Motorway Police (130) for road assistance.
  • In case of cybercrime, dial 1991 to report the issue.
  • If you encounter child safety concerns, reach out to the Child Protection Bureau at 1121.
  • For general telecommunications complaints, contact PTA at 0800-55055.

Having these numbers at hand ensures quick action and safety for everyone.
